About Us

Proper Auto has been in business since the Summer of 1999. With over 20 years of experience we have serviced a wide range of cars and left every customer feeling very happy with the services we have provided. We are renowned for our quick turnaround time and attention to detail.

We provide a car collection and drop off service. This allows our customers to have their car collected and dropped back at a location of their choice once we finish the service. This service is only applicable for locations within 10 kilometres of our centre.

According to customers, one of our favoured features is that we provide a car service warranty on any service received. Our car service warranty length is 6 months.

Why Choose Us?

Every member on our team at Proper Auto has extensive experience in every aspect of servicing cars. We only the hire the best of the best.

At Proper Auto, we are almost certain that you wont be quoted cheaper elsewhere. If you have found a cheaper quote we will match that price provided you show some official documentation of the quote given elsewhere.

If you dont have the time to get to our location a Proper Auto staff member will take the time to pick up your car and drop if back off after the servicing is complete so you wont even have to travel anywhere yourself to get the car serviced!. This service only applies to locations within 10 kilometres of our center.

At Proper Auto, we give a 6 month car service warranty warranty on all services carried out. This is the best vehicle warranty available for your car.

At Proper Auto we know that peoples schedule can be very hectic which is why our opening hours are 09:00 - 21:00 Monday to Friday.
