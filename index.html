About Us

Proper Auto has been in business since the Summer of 1999. With over 20 years of experience We have serviced countless numbers of cars and left every customer feeling very happy with the services we have provided. We are renowned for our quick turnaround time and attention to detail.

We provide a car collection and drop off service in which we can collect your car from any location within 10 kilometres of our location, drive it to our location, service it and then drop it back off at the location we picked it up.

Why Choose Us?

Every member on our team has extensive experience in every aspect of servicing cars

We are almost certain that you wont be quoted cheaper elesewhere. If you have found a cheaper quote we can match that price provided you show some official documentation of the quote given elsewhere.

If you dont have the time to get to our location we will take the time to pick up your car from any address within 10 kilometres of our centre and drop it back off at any location within 10 kilometres.

We give a 6 month warranty on all services.

We know that peoples schedule can be very hectic which is why our opening hours are 09:00 - 21:00 Monday to Friday.
