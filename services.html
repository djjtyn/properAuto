Clutch Replacement

Here at ProperAuto we know car clutches can be a bit of a hassle. At some point in your cars life the clutch is most likely going to malfunction which is why we offer a Clutch Replacement Service to our customers. There are a few sure signs your clutch needs to be replaced. We have listed just some of these:

  • If you are finding it difficult to change gears.
  • If the gears are changing by themselves.
  • If your car is revving hard going up hills.
  • If your car is revving harder than usual when accelarating
  • If your car is losing its speed while driving.

Pre NCT Checks

A Pre NCT Check is a great idea if you have your National Car Test(NCT) coming up and you have concerns about whether your car will pass or not.

The NCT is a compulsory vehicle inspection programme in Ireland. The main aim of the NCT is to improve road safety and ensure that all cars on the road are roadworthy and have no defects which may lead to an accident. For vehicles younger than 10 years, the NCT is conducted every 2 years and if the car is older than this the NCT is conducted annually

Our Pre NCT checks are free of charge and we will check wipers, all bulbs, tyre state, tyre pressure, brake pads and discs
